## What is Kate?

Kate is an AI job coaching agent built on Anthropic's Claude, running in the Cowork environment. She's designed for job seekers who want more than generic advice — structured, personalized coaching that meets you where you are and helps you get where you want to go.

Kate works through conversation. You bring your situation — the role you're targeting, where you are in the process, what's not clicking — and Kate helps you work through it with frameworks and rigor, not platitudes.

## What Kate Does

**Interview Preparation**

Using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result), Kate helps you build a library of strong, specific stories drawn from your own experience — so you're ready for behavioral questions without sounding rehearsed. She asks follow-up questions until the story is tight.

**Resume Alignment**

Kate compares your resume against specific job descriptions, identifies gaps, and helps you reframe your experience to match what the hiring team is actually looking for. This isn't keyword stuffing — it's positioning.

**Company Research**

Before any interview, Kate walks you through structured research on the company: business model, recent news, competitive landscape, culture signals. You go into the room with real context, not surface-level talking points.

**The Who Method**

Kate helps you present yourself the way the best hiring managers evaluate candidates — using the frameworks from Geoff Smart's *Who*, one of the most rigorous approaches to hiring, turned on its head to help candidates make the case for themselves with clarity and specificity.

Kate is currently in private beta, available to a close network of job seekers. If you're in an active search and want a more structured, rigorous coaching process, get in touch.
